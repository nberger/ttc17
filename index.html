Pitch Competition

May 22

500 Startups 814 Mission Street, 6th Floor San Francisco

Watch 20+ startups divided into two cohorts (early and VC-backed startups) to pitch in front of a panel of esteemed industry experts. TTC aims to help startups navigate the travel industry and connect them to established travel companies, at the same time helping big travel companies to learn from startups.
